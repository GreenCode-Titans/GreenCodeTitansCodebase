    • What is climate change?

    Climate change is a long-term shift in global or regional climate patterns. Climate change may cause weather patterns to be less predictable. A region might experience lower or higher than average temperatures. Climate change may cause more frequent and severe weather events, such as storms, floods, and droughts.

    The impacts of climate change

    • Rising sea levels
    • More extreme weather events
    • Changes in agricultural yields
    • Loss of biodiversity
    • Impacts on human health

    Solutions to climate change

    • Reduce greenhouse gas emissions
    • Invest in renewable energy
    • Improve energy efficiency
    • Protect forests
    • Adapt to the impacts of climate change
